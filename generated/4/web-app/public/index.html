Welcome to the Library Management System

This library management system is designed to help you manage your library books and users with ease. You can add new books to the system, track the borrowing and returning of books, and manage users. You can also search for books by title, author, or genre, and apply late fees for late returns.

To get started, click on one of the links in the navigation menu above.
